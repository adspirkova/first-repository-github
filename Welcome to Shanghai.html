Top 5 Sights

  1. The Bund

    Mainland China is most iconic concession-era backdrop, the Bund encapsulates Shanghai with its postcard-perfect good looks. A dazzling curve of bombastic masonry, it leads the way as one of the city is most stylish destinations.

  2. Shanghai Museum

    Shanghai is best museum by a long shot, this must-see is a marvellous tribute to the path of beauty throughout the millennia, from ancient bronzes and transcendent landscapes to gorgeous ceramic masterpieces.

  3. Yuyuan Gardens & Bazaar

    For some, the Old Town is beleaguered charms are one of the most alluring parts of a Shanghai visit. This classical garden is a fitting contrast to the city is future-now mantra.

  4. M50

    Located in a former cotton mill, the industrially chic M50 is the city is main creative hub. Dozens of edgy galleries and occasional events make this an absorbing place to wander.

  5. Jade Buddha Temple

    Despite first impressions, the city does have a strong current of religious tradition. It is best observed in this century-old Buddhist temple, which is witness to a continual stream of worshippers throughout the day.
